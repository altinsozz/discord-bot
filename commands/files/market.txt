**```ℹ️  | {{prefix}}market```**

Use:Used to perform several market commands.
Command:`{{prefix}}market price(s)/buy [id] [amount]/sell [id] [amount]`

Example:
`{{prefix}}market price(s)`-Shows a list of market prices.
`{{prefix}}market sell 1 3`-Sells 3 players with id 1.
`{{prefix}}market buy 2 5`-Buys 5 players with id 2.

```>Don't include brackets when using the commands.```
